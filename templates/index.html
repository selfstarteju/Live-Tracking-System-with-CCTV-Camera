How It Works

The Smart ID Card Tracking System uses your laptop's webcam to automatically scan ID cards and record attendance. Our system can read barcodes directly from the card strip or from anywhere on the card if the strip isn't clearly visible.

The system also extracts text information from the ID card and detects color indicators in the corner of the card to determine status. All this information is then logged to a secure database for reporting and analysis.

Designed for educational institutions, corporate offices, and event management, our solution offers a cost-effective and efficient way to monitor attendance and enhance security.
